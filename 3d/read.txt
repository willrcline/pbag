Holes in bezel for 12 x 12 x 4.3 mm TACT Switch Push Button.
Original bezel file found at https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:614241.
Bezel original file comes out in M and is scaled down to MM.
Perfboards are 6x4 cm. The space on each end is the same length as 2 hole units. There are 20 holes long on the perfboard. Therefore there are 12 total units(10 hole pairs and 2 end spots),
Each unit is therefore .5 cm (5mm)
